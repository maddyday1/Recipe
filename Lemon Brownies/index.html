Chewy Lemon Brownies

Prep Time
Cook Time
Total Time
20 MINS
50 MINS
70 MINS

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemond zest

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
  2. Beat flour, white sugar, butter, and sea salt together in a bowl using an electric mixer until well mixed.
  3. Whisk eggs, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, and 1 tablespoon lemon zest together in a seperate bowl
  4. mix into flour mixture and beat on medium speed until smooth, anout 3 minutes.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared baking dish.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 23 to 25 minutes.
  7. Cool until bottom of dish is warm to the touch
